# 0001 - Nextcloud Front Office

## Purpose

`Nextcloud` is the shared office.

It is the main human-facing and agent-visible workspace where work becomes
socially legible.

## Responsibilities

The front office owns:
- visible task movement
- readable plans and briefs
- durable collaborative knowledge
- office-style chatter
- human-visible accountability

## Expected Surfaces

The current expected surfaces are:
- Deck
- Collectives
- Talk
- Notes
- Tasks
- Calendar
- Contacts

## Non-Goals

The front office is not:
- the machine scheduler
- the lock or lease authority
- the canonical execution ledger
- the place where low-level runtime safety lives

## Main Boundaries

The front office speaks to:
- contributors
- AI pilots
- the Hive

It should remain the visible office, not the hidden machine core.
